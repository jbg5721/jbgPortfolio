Mazebound: The Process

This game was developed throughout the course of a semester (4 months time). I worked with a group of 4 other people with a variety of skills to plan, design, and develop Mazebound: The Shattered Portal.

My main role on this project was as an Artist. I created concept art, drafts, and final sprite sheets for a variety of different assets.
