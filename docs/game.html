Overview

Mazebound: The Shattered Portal

  • Role: Artist
  • Programs: Unity, Photoshop, & Pixel Studio
  • Game Concept: The game has a pixel art style with a top-down 2.5 view, a high fantasy theme and accompanied by a dark, moody, and somber tone. The player starts in an unknown location and the goal is to venture out into the three different mazes to progress the story to find answers and eventually escape.
